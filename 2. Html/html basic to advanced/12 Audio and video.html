Audio and video in html 

The html audio and video tags make it simple to add media to a website. you need to set src attributes to identify the
media source and include a controls attribute so the user can play and pause the media. use loop attribute to play 
audio continuously.use autoplay attribute to play the audio automatically, if your open the websites. notepad do not support the autoplay
 attribute. use width and height also.put into video starting tag inside only.
